About Us

Welcome to the Unitec Filipino Club, where we celebrate the rich and vibrant culture of the Philippines. Our club is a diverse and inclusive community that brings together students from all backgrounds to explore and embrace Filipino heritage. Through various cultural events, educational workshops, and social gatherings, we provide a platform for members to connect, learn, and share their love for Filipino traditions. Whether you're of Filipino descent or simply interested in the culture, you'll find a welcoming home in our club. Together, we aim to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation of our unique cultural identity, while creating lasting memories and friendships along the way.

Events

Our events are the heart and soul of the Unitec Filipino Club. We host a wide range of activities designed to celebrate our heritage, build community, and have a great time. From our signature Salo-Salo gatherings, where we come together to enjoy authentic Filipino cuisine, to our lively Filipino Movie Nights, there's always something exciting happening. We also organize traditional dance workshops, cooking classes, and language lessons to help members deepen their connection to Filipino culture. And, of course, we never miss a chance to celebrate major Filipino holidays and festivals, such as Buwan ng Wika and Christmas. No matter what event you attend, you're guaranteed to leave with a smile on your face and a stronger sense of belonging.

Join Us

Becoming a member of the Unitec Filipino Club is more than just joining a student organization—it's about being part of a family. We welcome students from all walks of life who are interested in experiencing and celebrating Filipino culture. As a member, you'll have access to exclusive events, workshops, and resources that will enhance your university experience. You'll also have the opportunity to make lifelong friends, develop leadership skills, and contribute to the vibrant Filipino community at Unitec. Membership is open year-round, so whether you're a new student or a returning one, there's always a place for you here. To join, simply reach out to us via email or attend our next event—our doors are always open!
